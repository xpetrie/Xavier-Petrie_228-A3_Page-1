Emotional Capture

This project, "Emotional Capture," required me to push past comfortable creative boundaries and translate an abstract feeling into a tangible, visceral object. I leveraged colour psychology, texture design, and physical discomfort to craft a prototype that is visually and physically unsettling. The raw, bleeding red and black texture, alongside the jagged, uncomfortable handle, creates a tactile experience mirroring the tension of rising rage.

Embody Aesthetics Posters

The task of this project was to visually articulate my core design principles using a personal aesthetic, with the end goal of creating four impactful visual posters. I successfully accomplished this by leveraging Adobe Express to transform abstract concepts into compelling pieces of art. This demonstrated my key skills in visual communication, aesthetic development, and effective use of digital design tools to produce cohesive, targeted visual outputs.

Journey of Design Magazine

The task was to evolve past my innate artistic skills and intentionally apply design principles to create a cohesive magazine. I accomplished this by using principles like balance and contrast to infuse the spreads with cinematic storytelling and a purposeful narrative. This project significantly enhanced my technical versatility in InDesign and Illustrator, specifically through the effective use of layered textures and complex, gritty compositions. My critical reflection ensured the final product successfully balanced challenging aesthetics with the structural demands of the magazine format, demonstrating proficiency and purpose.
